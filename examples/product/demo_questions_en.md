# Product Demo Questions (English)

Use these questions in your hackathon demo to show retrieval quality, join reasoning,
and business analytics on a US-market e-commerce dataset.

## Starter Questions

1. Show the top 10 best-selling products by units sold in the last 30 days.
2. What was total gross merchandise value (GMV) by channel (web, mobile, marketplace) last month?
3. List all orders from California customers in the last 14 days with order status and total amount.
4. Which customers placed more than 3 orders in the last 90 days?
5. What is the average order value by customer segment?

## Revenue and Margin Questions

6. Which product categories generated the highest net revenue this quarter?
7. Show monthly revenue, discount, tax, and shipping fee trends from September 2025 to January 2026.
8. What are the top 5 products by gross margin dollars?
9. Compare gross margin percentage for Apple vs Samsung products.
10. Which states contributed the most net sales in the holiday period (Nov 24 to Dec 24, 2025)?

## Operations and Logistics Questions

11. What is the on-hand inventory and reserved inventory by warehouse for each product?
12. Which products are below reorder point in any warehouse?
13. What is the average delivery time (in hours) by carrier?
14. Compare shipping cost per order for ground vs two_day vs overnight service.

## Customer Experience Questions

15. What is the return rate by product category?
16. Which products have an average review rating below 4.0?
17. What are the top return reasons and their refunded amounts?
18. Among VIP customers, who has the highest lifetime spend?
